✦ OUR STORY ✦

Rooted in nature.
Crowned in glory.

We believe every crown — every curl, coil, and kink — deserves to be celebrated.

2019

From a kitchen table to your crown

Naturally Crowned started in a small kitchen in Gaborone, with a simple belief: our hair shouldn't be a struggle. After years of experimenting with store-bought products filled with ingredients we couldn't pronounce, founder Thandiwe began crafting her own blends.

What started as gifts for friends and family quickly grew into a movement. Word spread. Women saw results. And Naturally Crowned was born.
